## General Design Guidelines for All Images

- **Font:** Use professional, readable sans-serif fonts (e.g., Inter, Open Sans, Montserrat)
- **Consistency:** Maintain consistent icon style and colour palette across all images
- **Branding:** Use VacatAd green (#DBF4CC) and dark grey (#232523) where appropriate
- **Readability:** Ensure text is large enough to read at web sizes (minimum 14px)
- **Accessibility:** Maintain good contrast ratios (WCAG AA compliant)
- **Format:** Export as WebP format, optimized for web (under 200KB each if possible)
