Navigating the Void: How Vacatad Revolutionises Relief for Empty Commercial Properties

By VacatAd Team 1 April 2023 2 min read
Innovation Relief Options Community Benefits VacatAd Services

Vacatad revolutionises commercial property management by transforming empty spaces into opportunities for savings and community benefit through their innovative, hassle-free approach to business rates relief.


Transforming the Landscape of Commercial Property Management

In the fast-paced realm of property management, commercial landlords are confronted with significant challenges, especially when dealing with empty properties. Vacatad emerges as a beacon of innovation, offering a unique, technology-driven solution to the financial burdens posed by business rates on vacant property, easing the load with minimal effort from the owners.

Vacatad’s Philosophy: Simplifying Rates Relief

Vacatad’s ethos is centred around a passion for aiding property owners in protecting their investments against the financial challenges of unoccupied spaces. Their technology-first strategy not only simplifies securing maximum rates relief but ensures a hassle-free experience for all involved parties.

Tailored Strategies for Property Owners

Vacatad encourages property owners to leverage their Rates Saving Consultation, a proactive measure designed to efficiently maximise savings and shield properties from the economic impact of vacancies. This approach reflects Vacatad’s commitment to providing custom solutions that cater to the individual needs of each client.

A Hassle-Free, Non-Invasive Method

Standing apart from traditional rates mitigation strategies, Vacatad’s method is noteworthy for its simplicity and ease of execution. The plug-and-play approach, requiring minimal logistical input, is both effective and gentle on the property. With Vacatad at the helm of communications with local authorities, property owners are freed from bureaucratic entanglements, allowing them to focus on more strategic property management aspects.

Beneficial Occupation: A Win-Win

The core of Vacatad’s service lies in the beneficial occupation concept. By taking control of properties for a six-week period and deploying their innovative solution, Vacatad not only aids in reducing business rates but also enriches the community with initiatives like free internet access and support for charitable activities. This dual-benefit strategy not only ensures beneficial occupation status but also maintains flexibility for property owners to find long-term tenants, striking a perfect balance between immediate relief and long-term planning.

Sustainable Relief and Value Delivery

In instances where properties remain vacant, Vacatad’s model supports the initiation of a re-occupation cycle, enabling ongoing savings on business rates through repeated beneficial occupation periods. This sustainable approach, paired with a "Pay only when you Save" pricing model, emphasises Vacatad’s dedication to providing value to their clients without demanding upfront costs, marking a significant shift in the management of commercial real estate towards efficiency, simplicity, and community benefit.
